## Overview

GetUpSoft's brand voice reflects **who we are**: a technical, trustworthy, enterprise-focused company that builds intelligent automation solutions. This guide ensures consistency across all written content — website copy, forms, emails, documentation, and marketing materials.

**Core Brand Personality:**
- **Technical** — Speak the language of architecture, integration, automation
- **Trustworthy** — Never oversell; back claims with facts
- **Enterprise-focused** — B2B mindset; respect complex business needs
- **Clear & Direct** — No marketing fluff; say what you mean
- **Bilingual** — ES and EN equally important; no machine translation

## 1. Tone Guidelines

### What We Sound Like

| Tone | Example | Context |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Authoritative** | "We architect enterprise digital ecosystems." | Hero statements, positioning |
| **Helpful** | "Here's how to assess your ERP readiness." | Guides, diagnostic content |
| **Technical** | "ORCA orchestrates agents, APIs, and workflows." | Product features, architecture |
| **Human** | "Let's talk about what's keeping your team up at night." | Contact, engagement CTAs |
| **Professional** | "20 years of enterprise integration experience." | Credentials, social proof |

### What We DON'T Sound Like

| Tone | Example | Context |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Hype** | "Revolutionary game-changing AI magic." | ❌ Never; too vague and untrustworthy |
| **Overpromising** | "Guaranteed 100% improvement in 30 days." | ❌ Never; cannot guarantee results |
| **Patronizing** | "It's so easy, even a non-tech person can do it!" | ❌ Never; assumes low audience capability |
| **Informal/Slangy** | "Yo, check out our sick automation tool bro!" | ❌ Never; not our audience or market |
| **Passive/Vague** | "Solutions for various enterprise challenges." | ❌ Never; be specific and direct |

## 2. Vocabulary Guide

### **Preferred** (Use These)

These words reflect our technical, professional identity:

#### Core Concepts
- **Architecture** (not "solution" or "platform")
- **Integrate** / **Integration** (not "connect" or "sync")
- **Automate** / **Automation** (not "simplify" or "optimize")
- **Orchestrate** / **Orchestration** (not "manage" or "coordinate")
- **Autonomous agents** (not "AI helpers" or "smart bots")
- **Operational Intelligence** (not "insights" or "data")
- **Enterprise** (not "business" or "company")
- **Scalable** (not "flexible" or "powerful")

#### Technical Language
- **API, integration layer, middleware** (not "connector" or "bridge")
- **ERP, CRM, data warehouse** (use full names first time, acronym after)
- **Cloud infrastructure, DevOps, microservices** (be specific)
- **Audit, design, deliver, scale** (our methodology)
- **ORCA, AIHub, GetUpBuilder** (always exact product names)
- **Odoo** (primary ERP; mention others only as "integrable")

#### Action Language
- **Deploy** (not "launch" or "roll out")
- **Implement** (not "put in place" or "execute")
- **Assess** / **Evaluate** (not "check out" or "look at")
- **Optimize** (not "improve" or "make better")
- **Monitor** / **Track** (not "keep an eye on")

#### Geographic Language (Dominican Republic)
- **República Dominicana** or **RD** (not "Dominican market" or "Caribbean region")
- **DGII** (Dirección General de Impuestos Internos — tax authority)
- **e-CF** (electronic invoicing standard)
- **Soporte Local** (Local Support; emphasize geographic proximity)

### **Forbidden** (Never Use These)

These words are either too vague, too hype-y, or contradict our brand:

- ❌ **"Best", "unique", "only"** → Can be disputed; removes specificity
- ❌ **"Perfect", "ideal", "amazing"** → Vague marketing speak
- ❌ **"Simple", "easy", "just"** → Patronizes technical audiences
- ❌ **"Revolutionary", "game-changing", "breakthrough"** → Hype; untrustworthy
- ❌ **"Guarantee", "promise", "always"** → Cannot guarantee results
- ❌ **"World-class", "leading", "innovative"** → Too generic
- ❌ **"Cutting-edge", "state-of-the-art"** → Overused; not credible
- ❌ **"Magic", "magical"** → Implies lack of understanding
- ❌ **"Seamless", "invisible"** → False; integration always has complexity
- ❌ **"One-click"** → Often not realistic; sets wrong expectations
- ❌ **"ROI", "savings"** → Only if specific and verified
- ❌ **"Maximize", "minimize"** → Vague; use percentages instead
- ❌ **"Empower", "enable"** → Overused startup jargon (unless specific: "enables Odoo adoption")

## 3. Grammar & Style Rules

### Capitalization

- **Product names:** Always capitalized
  - ✅ "ORCA Orchestrator"
  - ✅ "AIHub"
  - ✅ "GetUpBuilder"
  - ✅ "Integration Layer"

- **Features/Services:** Capitalized at start of sentence or list
  - ✅ "Cloud Infrastructure" (section title)
  - ✅ "Odoo ERP" (proper name)
  - ✅ "Our infrastructure services include: Cloud deployment, Security, DevOps, Support"

- **Framework names:** Title case for first mention, normal case after
  - ✅ "React 18 framework, using React for..." (after first mention)
  - ✅ "TailwindCSS for responsive design, with Tailwind utilities..."

### Numbers & Percentages

- **Exact metrics:** Use numbers if verified
  - ✅ "Reduce invoice processing time by 60–80%" (verified estimate range)
  - ✅ "Process 500+ documents daily" (with proof)

- **Vague numbers:** Avoid unless necessary
  - ❌ "Improve efficiency by up to 10X" (unverified; too vague)
  - ✅ "Increase document throughput from 50 to 500+ per day" (specific)

- **Ranges:** Use en-dash (–) not hyphen (-)
  - ✅ "60–80% reduction"
  - ✅ "1–3 week implementation"

### Sentence Structure

- **Active voice preferred:**
  - ✅ "We automate your invoice processing."
  - ❌ "Invoice processing is automated by our system."

- **Short, direct sentences:**
  - ✅ "ORCA orchestrates agents. Agents automate workflows. You gain visibility."
  - ❌ "Our orchestration platform, which manages autonomous agents that are responsible for automating various workflows, provides visibility."

- **Avoid "we" overuse:**
  - ✅ "GetUpSoft deploys autonomous agents." (use company name sometimes)
  - ✅ "You'll see results in weeks." (focus on customer)
  - ❌ "We believe we build the best we offer we are we we we..." (repetitive)

### Lists & Formatting

- **Bulleted lists:** Use when items are independent
  - ✅ "Our capabilities:"
    - ✅ Autonomous AI agents
    - ✅ ERP integration layer
    - ✅ Cloud infrastructure design

- **Numbered lists:** Use for sequential steps
  - ✅ "Our methodology:"
    1. ✅ Architecture Audit
    2. ✅ Intelligence Design
    3. ✅ Operational Delivery
    4. ✅ Scale & Support

## 4. Writing by Content Type

### Hero Headlines & CTAs

**Purpose:** Grab attention, communicate main value, motivate action

**Guidelines:**
- **Length:** 5–10 words for headlines; 2–4 words for CTAs
- **Verb-first:** Start with action (if appropriate)
- **Specificity:** Name the benefit or outcome
- **Tone:** Authoritative, confident, clear

**Examples:**

| ✅ Good | ❌ Bad | Why |
|--------|--------|-----|
| "Scalability and intelligence for the modern enterprise" | "Solutions for your business" | Specific vs. vague |
| "Automate everything" | "Next-generation platforms" | Clear vs. hype |
| "Book Strategy Session" | "Learn More" | Action-oriented vs. generic |
| "Assess Your ERP Readiness" | "Get Started" | Specific call-to-action |

### Body Copy (Paragraphs)

**Purpose:** Explain features, justify claims, build trust

**Guidelines:**
- **Length:** 2–4 sentences per paragraph (mobile-first)
- **Lead with benefit:** "Here's what you get..."
- **Support with detail:** "How it works..."
- **Include social proof:** "Why it matters..."
- **End with action:** "Next step..."

**Template:**
```
[BENEFIT] GetUpSoft automates invoice processing using AI agents.
[DETAIL] ORCA extracts data, validates formats, and submits to your ERP automatically.
[PROOF] Reduces manual work by 80%, freeing your team for strategic work.
[ACTION] Assess your readiness with our free diagnostic.
```

### Form Copy (Labels, Placeholders, Help Text)

**Purpose:** Guide user input; minimize friction; communicate requirements

**Guidelines:**
- **Labels:** Clear, specific, verb-based where possible
  - ✅ "What is your current ERP?" (specific)
  - ❌ "ERP" (vague)
  - ✅ "Company name"
  - ❌ "Organization / Business entity / Legal name" (too much choice)

- **Placeholders:** Contextual hints, not instructions
  - ✅ "john.doe@company.com"
  - ❌ "Enter your email address here"

- **Help text:** Clarify if needed; be brief
  - ✅ "We'll use this to identify your organization in our system."
  - ❌ "This is required because we need to know who you are for various purposes including billing and support."

- **Error messages:** Actionable, not blaming
  - ✅ "Email required. Please enter a valid email address."
  - ❌ "You forgot your email" or "Invalid input"
  - ✅ "Minimum 10 characters for company name"
  - ❌ "Your answer is too short"

### Bilingual (Spanish & English)

**For Spanish content:**
- **Terminology:** Use consistent Spanish-language technical terms
  - ✅ "Agentes de IA" (not "AI agents" in Spanish text)
  - ✅ "Orquestación" (not "Orchestration")
  - ✅ "Arquitectura" (not "Architecture")

- **Avoid machine translation:**
  - ❌ "Automatizar todo lo" (grammatically incorrect)
  - ✅ "Automatiza todo" (correct Spanish command form)

- **Tone alignment:** Spanish copy should match English tone (professional, technical, direct)
  - ✅ ES: "Implementamos Odoo ERP, facturación electrónica..." (matches EN authoritative tone)
  - ❌ ES: "¡Hola amigos! Nuestro increíble software..." (too casual; not our voice)

- **Regional specificity:** RD content is Spanish-only; use local terminology
  - ✅ "DGII / e-CF compliance" (Dominican tax system)
  - ✅ "Soporte local RD" (Local RD support)
  - ✅ "Infraestructura empresarial" (Enterprise infrastructure)

## 6. Content Enforcement

### Before Publication

Every piece of published content must pass:

1. **Tone check:**
   - Does it sound like GetUpSoft? (Technical, trustworthy, clear?)
   - Are forbidden words avoided? (No "revolutionary", "best", "guarantee"?)

2. **Accuracy check:**
   - Is every factual claim verified in docs/content-source-map.md?
   - Are technical details accurate?
   - Is there no invented data?

3. **Clarity check:**
   - Can a non-native English speaker understand?
   - Are sentences short and direct?
   - Is active voice used?

4. **Brand consistency:**
   - Does it align with vision in master prompt §1?
   - Does it respect Odoo as primary ERP?
   - Does it match the tone of other pages?

### Who Enforces

- **ChatGPT (Content Writer):** Writes copy; self-reviews against this guide
- **Claude (Editor):** Reviews all copy before publication; rejects or requests revisions
- **Joel (Product Owner):** Final approval for claims and strategic positioning

### Revision Workflow

If copy is rejected:
1. **Specific feedback:** "This uses 'revolutionary' (forbidden); revise to 'advanced' or 'powerful'"
2. **Revision deadline:** 24 hours
3. **Re-review:** Ensure feedback addressed
4. **Approval:** Once corrections made, publish

## 7. Glossary of Key Terms

| Term | Definition | Usage |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **ORCA** | Orchestration platform for autonomous agents | "ORCA orchestrates your AI agents" |
| **Autonomous Agents** | AI systems that operate independently based on rules | "Autonomous agents process invoices without manual input" |
| **ERP** | Enterprise Resource Planning system (Odoo, ERPNext, etc.) | "Integrate your ERP with ORCA" |
| **Orchestration** | Coordination of multiple systems, agents, or processes | "ORCA orchestrates your entire tech stack" |
| **Integration** | Connection between two or more systems | "Our integration layer connects Odoo, CRM, BI tools" |
| **Cloud Infrastructure** | Scalable computing, storage, networking in the cloud | "We design cloud infrastructure for high-traffic apps" |
| **Operational Intelligence** | Insights from real-time business process data | "Operational intelligence enables faster decisions" |
| **Architecture** | Overall structure and design of systems | "We audit your architecture and recommend improvements" |
| **Automation** | Use of technology to perform tasks without manual intervention | "Automation reduces manual data entry by 80%" |
| **Audit** | Comprehensive assessment of current systems | "We start with a thorough architecture audit" |
| **DGII** | Dominican tax authority; requires electronic invoicing | "DGII compliance is mandatory in Dominican Republic" |
| **e-CF** | Electronic invoicing format in Dominican Republic | "Odoo supports e-CF invoicing for RD compliance" |
