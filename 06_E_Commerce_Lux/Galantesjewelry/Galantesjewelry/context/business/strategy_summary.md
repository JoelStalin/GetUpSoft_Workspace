# Business Strategy Summary

## Objective
Convert mobile/web visitors—especially high-value tourists, locals, and those planning destination weddings—into confirmed private appointments or consultations.

## Channel Strategy
- **Primary Digital Channels**: Local SEO, Google Business Profile, Instagram Business, and Meta Business.
- **Tactics**: Geofencing, remarketing, and clienteling (building long-term relationships through high-touch service).
- **CTA Focus**: "Book a Private Appointment", "Schedule a Consultation".

## Roadmap
1. Core Branding & Content
2. High-performance mobile-first web launch
3. Local SEO Optimization & Schema setup
4. Instagram/Meta integration & remarketing launch
5. Clienteling and database nurturing (Reputation & Reviews)
