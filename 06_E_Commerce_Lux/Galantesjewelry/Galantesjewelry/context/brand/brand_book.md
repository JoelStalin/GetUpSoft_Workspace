# Brand Book: Galante's Jewelry by the Sea

## Identity
- **Name**: Galante's Jewelry by the Sea
- **Positioning**: The Coastal Concierge. Providing luxury, high-end, bespoke nautical jewelry and specialized services.
- **Location**: Islamorada, Florida Keys.
- **Aesthetic**: Barefoot luxury. Refined but relaxed coastal elegance.

## Colors & Typography
- **Primary Color**: Deep Marine Blue (#002366)
- **Accent Color**: Sun-kissed Gold (#D4AF37)
- **Typography**: Editorial luxury serif for headings; clean sans-serif for UI and body text.

## Core Services
1. Nautical Gold & Sterling Silver Collections
2. Bridal & Destination Weddings Jewelry
3. Premium Watch & Jewelry Repair

## Visual Guidelines
- White space must be generous.
- Interfaces must be high-contrast and legible in harsh sunlight (tropical environment).
- Photography should evoke the heritage of the sea and the craftsmanship of fine jewelry.
