# Voice and Tone

## Core Tone
The tone is sophisticated, cálido (warm), local, and concierge-level. It should read like an expert local jeweler who knows the area and crafts pieces meant to last generations.

## Do's
- Speak to exclusivity, craftsmanship, memory, heritage, and destination.
- Be helpful and consultative ("Private Consultation", "Book a Private Appointment").
- Emphasize the unique nature of Islamorada and the Florida Keys.
- Use words like: bespoke, heirloom, coastal, concierges, curated.

## Don'ts
- Do not sell strictly on price (avoid "cheap", "bargain", "discount").
- Do not use overly aggressive marketing language.
- Do not sound mass-market or generic.
