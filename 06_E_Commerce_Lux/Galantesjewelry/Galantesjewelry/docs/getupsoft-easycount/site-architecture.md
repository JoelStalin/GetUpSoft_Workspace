# Corporate Web Information Architecture

## GetUpSoft Web

- Home: corporate positioning and primary service categories.
- Services: software development, infrastructure, automation, Odoo integration.
- Industries/Cases: MIPYMES, retail, services, operations-heavy businesses.
- Products: EasyCount by GetUpSoft and future product lines.
- Contact: consultation request and business intake.

## EasyCount Product Web

- Overview: operational problem and product promise.
- Features: counting, invoicing, sync status, reporting, Odoo integration.
- Integrations: Odoo first, payment/accounting integrations later.
- Pricing: phased placeholder until packaging is approved.
- Support: help, contact, status.

## Navigation Rule

Corporate pages should lead with GetUpSoft. Product pages should lead with EasyCount while preserving `by GetUpSoft` trust signals in footer, legal, and onboarding contexts.
