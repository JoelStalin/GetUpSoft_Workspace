# Brand Guidelines v1

## Architecture

- Master brand: GetUpSoft.
- Product brand: EasyCount by GetUpSoft.
- Public product shorthand: EasyCount.
- Legal/vendor trust contexts: EasyCount by GetUpSoft.

## Positioning

GetUpSoft builds software, infrastructure, and automation systems for MIPYMES that need reliable operations without enterprise complexity.

EasyCount is the GetUpSoft product line for operational counting, billing, and Odoo-backed business workflows.

## Voice

- Clear, practical, and business-focused.
- Avoid hype and vague AI/productivity claims.
- Explain operational value in concrete outcomes: fewer manual steps, cleaner records, faster reconciliation, better visibility.

## Co-Branding Rules

- Use `EasyCount by GetUpSoft` on first mention, legal pages, pricing, invoices, and partner-facing materials.
- Use `EasyCount` alone inside product navigation after brand context is established.
- Use GetUpSoft as the authority brand on corporate pages and integration/security documentation.
